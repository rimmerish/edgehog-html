EH-01

A compact experimental sampler synth. No menus. No LEDs. No screen. Just control.

SD + memory playback, glitch, loop, morph and repeat in a small hands-on instrument built for immediate noise, rhythm and strange little moments.

What it does

Small box. Direct controls. Weird results.

Built around immediacy: buttons, knobs, samples, synth voices, loops and performance accidents that are actually useful.

01

Memory samples

Fast onboard sample sets for drums, vocal textures, glitches and one-shot fragments.

02

SD playback

Stream longer files from SD, hold loops, freeze moments and turn samples into playable material.

03

Synth engine

Polyphonic digital synthesis with filter, envelopes and modulation under physical control.

04

USB MIDI

Send notes, receive notes, map controls and use EH-01 with a DAW or browser companion tool.

05

No menus

The interface is built around reach-and-turn decisions, not tiny screens and nested settings.

06

Open-ended

Designed as a playable instrument first, but with room for firmware updates and curious modification.

A strange little instrument from a slightly more useful timeline.
